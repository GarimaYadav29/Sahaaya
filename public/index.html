" Connecting surplus resources to those in need "

What is Sahaaya ?

Sahaaya is an initiative aimed at bridging the gap between those with surplus resources, such as food, clothing, and books, and those in need. Our mission is to reduce waste and contribute to the welfare of society by efficiently distributing these resources. We strive to create a network of donors and recipients to ensure that resources are used in the best possible way to benefit the underprivileged.

How would you like to proceed ?

Sahaaya is committed to connecting surplus resources with those in need, contributing to a more sustainable and equitable society.
