About Us

Sahaaya is an initiative designed to bring positive change by connecting resources that would otherwise go to waste with individuals and communities that need them the most. Our platform facilitates the seamless distribution of surplus food, clothing, and books to underprivileged sections of society.

Our goal is to reduce waste while creating an ecosystem of sharing and support. With the combined effort of our donors and recipients, we aim to build a more equitable and sustainable society.

Our Vision

To create a world where no resource goes to waste and everyone has access to essential needs.

Our Mission

To connect surplus resources to those in need, fostering a culture of sharing and mutual support.
